Taiwanese Stinky Tofu

臭豆腐
Stinky tofu is a classic in night markets it is a type of fermented tofu with a strong smell, it is definitely an acquired taste for some people. Personally, I think the taste overpowers the smell, and I always have to get it at least once when I visit Taiwan.

Shui jian Bao

水煎包
Shui Jian Bao is a pan-fried bun with crispy, golden brown outside and fully, favorful filling inside. It can be made with vegetable or meat fillings. You can have them anytime of the day but they are always made fresh at the stall.
